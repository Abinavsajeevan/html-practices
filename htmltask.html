Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an Indian scientist, statesman, and the 11th President of India (2002-2007). He is known as the "Missile Man of India" for his role in India's missile and nuclear programs. He was a respected figure, particularly among young people, and was known for his inspirational speeches and writings.
